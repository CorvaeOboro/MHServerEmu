Server Mods

Loot Filter

Per-player rarity filtering for unwanted drops. Set thresholds by item type globally or per hero.

Auto-Pickup Items

Automatically picks up currency, crafting mats, relics, and runes from the ground near your avatar.

Orb Pickup Radius

Widens orb auto-magnet range so XP, credits, health, and endurance orbs pull in from much farther away.

Chest Auto-Open

Automatically opens chests, crates, and gift boxes in general inventory.

Throwable Disable

Prevents the animation lock from picking up and throwing objects like cars and rocks.

Interact Nearby Auto

Auto-clicks mission objectives, civilians, and eligible interactables as you walk within range.

DangerRoom Combine

Combines pairs of Danger Room scenario portals into higher-tier ones.

Incursion

Spawns hostile Hero Variant invaders that hunt players in combat regions. 60+ enemy types.

Client Mods

Display Message Removal

Suppresses on-screen power failure messages like "Power Not Ready" .

Donate Without Confirm

Removes the confirmation popup when donating items .

Sound Silencer

Mutes unwanted voicelines and sound effects .
