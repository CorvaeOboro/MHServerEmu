Incursion

Hostile Hero Variant invaders that hunt players in combat regions

Incursion spawns enemy heroes that look and fight like playable characters. They appear randomly ( every 3-9min ) while you are fighting in non-hub regions and actively chase you down.

  • 60+ enemy types, one per playable hero
  • They use a subset of that hero's avatar powers
  • Balanced to be dangerous but short-lived
  • See through invisibility

Commands

CommandDescription
!incursion nowSpawn a random invader immediately
!incursion spawn <name>Spawn a specific invader
!incursion startEnable spawning
!incursion stopDisable spawning
!incursion statusShow current settings
!incursion trialStart a 1v1 gauntlet against all enemy types
!incursion trial stopEnd the trial
