Chest Auto-Open

Automatically opens chests and boxes in your inventory

A server-side loop that automatically opens chest-type items sitting in your general inventory.

Every few seconds the server scans your general inventory for items matching the whitelist. Matching items are opened automatically on a cooldown.

Config Options

OptionDefaultDescription
ItemChestAutoOpenEnabletrueMaster switch
ItemChestAutoOpenCooldownMs1100Milliseconds between opens (min 500)
ItemChestAutoOpenWhitelistChest,Crate,LootBox,Giftbox,GiftBoxComma-separated name patterns to open
ItemChestAutoOpenLoggingEnablefalseDebug logging
